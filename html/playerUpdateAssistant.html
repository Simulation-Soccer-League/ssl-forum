How to use this assistant?

The Build Assistant allows you to test various builds for your player. You can select your (or any) player in the drop-down menu. Once you are finished with your build you can click on 'Summarize your update' and a popup will appear with your formatted update text. Clicking on 'Copy text' will copy the text to your cliboard to save for later.
