How to use the Update Assistant?

The Update Assistant automatically selects your player if you are logged in to the forum. You can select your player in the drop-down menu if it is not done automatically.

To get a full update output, you can enter in your unclaimed earnings in the list at the top of the page, clicking on '+ Row' to add more rows in case you need them. The Earned TPE will automatically sum based on your earned TPE for each claim. If you do not want to use the 'link collector' you can enter in the total earned TPE manually.

Finally after all your earned TPE have been added, you may start to increase your attributes. Once you are finished you can click on 'Summarize your update' and a popup will appear with your formatted update text. Clicking on 'Copy text' will copy the text to your cliboard which you then can paste as a new post in your update thread.
