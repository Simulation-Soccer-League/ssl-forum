How to use the assistant?

Throughout your player's career, you can make purchases in the Player Store using your earned money. Each purchase must be posted in the Store Purchases thread and be approved by an Banker before you are able to claim your purchase on your update page.

This page allows you to look through the purchaseable items and select what you want to purchase and then exporting a correctly formatted text that can be copied in the Store thread. If you are logged in on the forum, the Store will also automatically read your current bank status and tell you if you do not have coverage for your purchase.

Use the "+Row" to add additional purchases from a category.

Individual training

You can purchase an individual training package in five different levels:

  • Sunday League ($1.5 million) - 1 TPE
  • Amateur League ($3 million) - 4 TPE
  • Professional League ($4.5 million) - 8 TPE
  • Superstar League ($5.5 million) - 12 TPE
  • World Class League ($7.5 million) - 18 TPE
There is no limit to the amount of individual trainings you can purchase during a season.

Traits

Each new trait costs $3 million and your player can have a maximum of 7 traits, including the 2 you started with.

The traits are separated into seven different categories depending on what part of the play they affect. Note that some of the traits will negatively impact your player if their attributes does not match.

If you notice a disabled trait in the list, it is not compatible with one or more of your current traits. The full list of incompatible traits can be found in the Player Guide.

Weak Foot

The footedness of a player is split into a main (20) and weak (10) foot based on the preferred footedness of the player selected at creation. Purchasing this item ($7.5 million) gives you an additional 5 XP on your weaker foot.

Positional Experience

At creation, a player starts with one primary and two secondary positions. With purchases in the Player Store you are able to switch, upgrade or add positions to your player.

The items available are:

  • Position Transfer ($5 million): You need to indicate in your subsequent update post which two positions you want to transfer the experience between. For example switching from CD -> CDM.
  • Position Upgrade ($7.5 million): You need to indicate in your subsequent update post which of your current secondary positions you want to upgrade to a primary position.
  • Position Addition ($12.5 million): You need to indicate in your subsequent update post which position you want to add as an additional secondary position.
