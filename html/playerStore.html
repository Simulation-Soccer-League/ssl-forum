Player Store

Throughout your player's career, you can make purchases in the Player Store using your earned money. Each purchase must be posted in the Store thread and be approved by an Accountant before you are able to claim your purchase on your update page.

This page allows you to look through the purchaseable items and select what you want to purchase and then exporting a correctly formatted text that can be copied in the Store thread. If you are logged in on the forum, the Store will also automatically read your current bank status and tell you if you do not have coverage for your purchase.

Traits

You can also choose to purchase additional traits for your player. Each trait costs $3 million and your player can have a maximum of 7 traits, including the 2 you started with. The traits are separated into seven different types of traits depending on what part of the play they affect. Note that some of the traits will negatively impact your player if their attributes does not match.

# Purchase Cost
1

Remove a current trait

Removing a player trait costs $500 000.
