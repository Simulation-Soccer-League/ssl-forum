Player Store

Throughout your player's career, you can make purchases in the Player Store using your earned money. Each purchase must be posted in the Store thread and be approved by an Accountant before you are able to claim your purchase on your update page.

This page allows you to look through the purchaseable items and select what you want to purchase and then exporting a correctly formatted text that can be copied in the Store thread. If you are logged in on the forum, the Store will also automatically read your current bank status and tell you if you do not have coverage for your purchase.

Traits

You can purchase additional traits for your player. Each trait costs $3 million and your player can have a maximum of 7 traits, including the 2 you started with.

The traits are separated into seven different types of traits depending on what part of the play they affect. Note that some of the traits will negatively impact your player if their attributes does not match.

If you notice a disabled trait in the list, it is not compatible with one or more of your current traits.

Positional Experience

At creation, a player starts with one primary and two secondary positions corresponding to 20 and 15 positional XP in Football Manager respectively.

You are able to either purchase a transfer of your current experience where you indicate in the update page which two positions you are switching the experience between. You are also able to purchase either an additional secondary position (15 XP) or upgrade a secondary position to an additional primary position (15 -> 20 XP).
